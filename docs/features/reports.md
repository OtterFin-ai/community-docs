# Reports

Reports give you a high-level view of your household's financial activity over time.

## Spending & Income

The **Spending & Income** report breaks down your income and spending by category across a selected date range. It shows:

- Total income for the period
- Total spending for the period
- Net change (income minus spending)
- A category-by-category breakdown of where money came in and went out

This report is the primary tool for understanding your financial patterns — where the most money is going, which categories are growing or shrinking over time, and how your income compares to your spending.

### Date range

You can select any date range for the report. Common presets (this month, last month, this year, last year) are available, as well as a custom range picker.

### Multi-currency handling

If your household uses multiple currencies, all amounts are converted to your household's base currency for the report. Conversion uses the exchange rate for each transaction's date, so the report reflects real values rather than an averaged rate.

Transactions where no exchange rate path is available are excluded from the totals rather than using a fallback that could distort the numbers.

### Transfers excluded

Transfers between accounts are excluded from all spending and income calculations. Moving money from your checking account to your savings account is not income or spending — only transactions with a category count.
